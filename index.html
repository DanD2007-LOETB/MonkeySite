What is This Website?

This website was made to provide information on different species of monkeys and provide facts that you can share with others, or be used for research. Throughout this website, hovering images may change how they appear.

The species included in this website are:

  • Gibbons
  • Macaques
  • Capuchin
  • Proboscis
  • Marmoset
